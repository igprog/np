NutriProg

Easy to use web application for creating balanced diet plan


Because of simplicity in the use the application is suitable not only for professionals like nutritionists and dietitians but also for everyone else who take care of their health and maintain an appropriate body weight. NutriProg is a web application so you do not need to install software on your computer, You just need to Sign up and then you can access the application from any device that has internet access.

NutriProg allows nutritionists to determine nutrition status and energy consumption of their patients and the creation of menus based on scientific knowledge in food and nutrition. By creating menus using this application, end-user nutrition becomes balanced and varied thanks to the fact that the menu design is based on the recommended number of unit serving of all food groups and their energy, macro and micronutrition values. Since the NutriProg contains a database of over 700 foods and meals, it can satisfy the demands of most users regarding food choice. In addition, the application provides the ability to enter your own foods and their macro and micronutritional values. Furthermore, when talking about the problem of overweight, it is recommended with changing dietary habits in everyday life to include additional physical activity. NutriProg provides information about the energy consumption of over 400 physical activities.
NutriProg helps nutritionists and dietitians to keep track of their patients and monitor their anthropometric parameters.
