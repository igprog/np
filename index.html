About Nutri Prog


Because of simplicity in the use the application is suitable not only for professionals but also for everyone else who take care of their health and maintain an appropriate body weight. Nutri Prog is a web application so you do not need to install software on your computer, You just need to Sign up and then you can access the application from any device that has internet access.

Nutri Prog allows to determine the state of nutrition and energy consumption of the individual and the creation of menus based on scientific knowledge in the field of nutrition. By creating menus using this application, end-user nutrition becomes balanced and varied thanks to the fact that the menu design is based on the recommended number of unit serving of all food groups and their energy, macro and micronutrition values. Since the application Nutri Prog contains a database of over 700 foods and meals, it can satisfy the demands of most users regarding food choice. In addition, the application provides the ability to enter your own foods and their macro and micronutritional values. Furthermore, when talking about the problem of overweight, it is recommended with changing dietary habits in everyday life to include additional physical activity. And so Nutri Prog provides information about the energy consumption of over 400 physical activities.
